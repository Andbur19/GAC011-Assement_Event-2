Mexican

The tacos restaurants or wagons where you can buy some tacos or quesadillas whatever you prefer these ones are in every corner of Culiacan so if you want to taste the best Mexican food go for some tacos and you can go to Piaxtla taqueria.

Japanese

The best Japanese restaurant here in Culiacan is yuniku, and its a specialized restaurant in Japanese food, which have a Japanese chef , so if you want to taste the flavour of Japan here in Culiacan go to yuniku.

Chinese

The best Chinese restaurant is Shangai, and it has dishes from soups to chicken dishes, if you want to experience the best Chinese food in Culiacan you must go there because no one has that flavour and we have gone to a lot but this one is the best.

Fast Food

The best Fast Food restaurant is Mcdonalds, it has the best meal and the best hamburgers when we talk about fast food, their flavour and its ingredients, combinations, the meat, and the french fries makes it the best one.
